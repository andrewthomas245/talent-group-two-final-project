Claim Procedures


Contact IG2 Insurance Claim Hotline 09 753010834 (24/7) or fill online proposal form and send to IG2 or IG2 authorized agents.

IG2 Insurance will inform to survey team and assign the way.

Survey team will visit the claim site and assess the damage if you are in damage or will verify your information if you are maturity.

Assessment team will be reported by survey team within 24 hours.

Claim settlement will be received within 5 working days after receiving the invoice.
