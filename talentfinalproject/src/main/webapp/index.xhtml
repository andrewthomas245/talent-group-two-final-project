SHORT TERM ENDOWMENT LIFE INSURANCE


Introduction

   Short term endowment life policy gives saving benefit, return on premium, tax exemption and protection against the financial loss incurred by the death or permanent disability of the person insured.

Purpose

  • To provide financial support in the case of unexpected tragedies.

  • To ensure that the certain amount of money are saved in the desired term.

Insurable Persons

  • Anyone who has insurable interest in his or her own life.

  • Parent who has insurable interest in the lives of his or her children.

  • Husband who has an insurable interest in the life of his spouse and vice versa.

Insurable Age

  • Between 10 to 60 years old.

    • (If the insured person is within 10 -18 years, the approval letter from their guardian will be needed.)

  • The person between the age of 10-18 years can insure with the parent's agreement.

Policy Terms

  • 5-years

  • 7-years

  • 10-years

Sum Insured

  • Minimum 1,000,000 kyats to 50,000,000 kyats.

Premium Payment

   Premium payment can be made monthly, quarterly, bi-annually, annually as per insured person’s preference.

Premium Rate

   Premium payment rate will be calculated based on insured's age, policy term ane sum insured of the insured person.

Advantages of short term endowment

  • Build a good saving habit.

  • Financial support for the family in unexpected tragedy.

  • Total Benefit/Maturity Benefit amount exceeds the premium paid.

  • Personal Income Tax saving.

  • No tax shall be assessed on insurance indemnity or maturity benefit.

  • No warrant from debtors is allowed to issue upon policy benefits.

Benefit

  • Maturity Benefit

    •   - Once the policy is mature, the insured can withdraw the sum assured in lump sum.

  • Death Benefit

    •   - If the insured die during the Policy term, sum assured will be paid to beneficiary.

  • Total Permanent Disability (TPD) Benefit

    •   - If the insured is permanently and totally disable due to accident or disease with total loss of earning capacity when the policy is in force, the sum assured will be paid in lump sum.

  • Paid-Up Policy

    •   - If the insured can no longer pay the premium while the Policy in force, the insured can change to the Paid-up policy.

      - Paid-up value will be provided to the insured on the mature data stated in the Policy.

      - If the insured died during the policy term, only Paid-up value will be provided to the beneficiary.

  • Surrender Value or Cash Value

    •   - Surrender value will be paid to the insured in accordance with the Surrender value rate table when the insured notified to surrender a Policy which is in foce to withdraw the Surrender value.

  • Policy Loan

    •   - The insured has the right to get a loan from a valid Policy.
        - The amount of loan is 90% of the Surrender value.

Exclusions

   The policy does not provide Death Benefit due to the following causes and only premium paid will be returned.

  • The occurrences of suicide within one year from policy start date.

  • Death cases happened within one year from the policy starting date and are caused by the existing diseases that are not disclosed intentionally by the policy holder.

   The policy does not provide Total Permanent Disability Benefit due to the following causes and only premium paid will be returned.

  • Total Permanent Disability caused intentionally by self-injury.

  • Total Permanent Disability caused by suicide.

  • Total Permanent Disability caused by abuse of drugs.

  • Total Permanent Disability caused by taking dangerous and risky medicine.

  • Total Permanent Disability caused by the criminal commitment.

  • Total Permanent Disability cases happened within one year from the policy starting date and are caused by the existing diseases that are not disclosed intentionally by the policy holder.
