An endowment policy is a life insurance contract designed to pay a lump sum after a specific term on its ‘maturity’ or unfortunate demise of the policy holder. In literal terms, the best suitable endowment policy will serve as both saving and protection. Such policies will provide maturity and death benefit both; The maturity benefit is payable if the policy holder survives until the end of the policy term and pays the entire premium.
